About Me

My love for data and telling stories with data was an unexpected discovery for me. I've always had a love for technology and once I discovered my passion for sports, I knew I wanted to find a way to combine these two interests. Going into undergrad undeclared provided me the opportunity to explore every route towards making my passions my career. This is where I discovered data analytics and how it can not only be applied to sports, but just exactly the impact it can make on teams when they choose to embrace it. I've always loved sports from a fan perspective, and this has allowed me to approach data with a unique perspective that is rooted in a deep understanding of the game and its nuances. My time with the Illini Pride Student Athletic Board under Orange Krush, Block I, and Spike Squad has provided me another unique perspective on the event coordination and fan engagement side of sports, which has allowed me to become a better analyst understanding all sides and aspects of the sports industry.

As I explored further into the world of data analytics, I found myself drawn to the healthcare industry as well. The potential for data to drive insights and improve patient outcomes is immense, and I wanted to be a part of that impact. My coursework under my Interdisciplinary Health Technology minor has provided me with a strong foundation in healthcare and the social determinants of health, driving me to my passion of making sure underserved communities have access to the care they need. I'm particularly interested in how data can be used to identify and address health disparities as well as how data can positively transform chronic disease management. I'm excited about the potential for data analytics to drive positive change in this area and see what the future holds at the intersection of healthcare and data analytics.

Outside of my academics, I enjoy working on personal projects that allow me to apply my skills in real-world scenarios. I'm also involved in various extracurricular activities that help me grow both personally and professionally. I believe in the power of collaboration and am always eager to learn from others all around me!

Education

Focus Areas

Sports Analytics and Healthcare Analytics

Career Goal

Aspiring Data Analyst dedicated to driving insights in sports and healthcare
