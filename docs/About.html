About Me

Hi y'all! I'm currently an undergraduate student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, studying Information Sciences with minors in Data Science and Recreation, Sport, and Tourism. I'm passionate about using data to uncover meaningful insights that can make a real impact in the sports and healthcare industries.

My journey into data analysis began with my love for sports and curiosity about the stories that numbers can tell and how these stories impact the game. I'm fascinated with the ways data can reveal patterns, predict outcomes, and drive decision-making in ways that can improve both athletic performance and healthcare outcomes.

Outside of my academics, I enjoy working on personal projects that allow me to apply my skills in real-world scenarios. I'm also involved in various extracurricular activities that help me grow both personally and professionally. I believe in the power of collaboration and am always eager to learn from others all around me!
