About Me

Hi! I'm an undergraduate student at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, studying Information Sciences with minors in Data Science and Recreation, Sport, and Tourism. I'm passionate about leveraging data to uncover meaningful insights that can make a real impact in the sports and healthcare industries.

My journey into data analysis began with my love for sports and curiosity about the stories that numbers can tell. I'm fascinated by how data can reveal patterns, predict outcomes, and drive decision-making in ways that can improve both athletic performance and healthcare outcomes.

When I'm not diving into datasets or coding, you can find me exploring the latest trends in sports analytics, staying active, or working on personal projects that combine my technical skills with my interests in recreation and tourism.
